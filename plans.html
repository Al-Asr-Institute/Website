Beginners
$15 per session

The Quranic Arabic course focuses on teaching students the most common words in the Quran. Students will build up their vocabulary step-by-step, to the point that one day they will realize they understand what the Imam is reciting in Salah!

Tajweed

Beginners
$25 per session
Prerequesites: Must have the ability to read the Quran
The Beginners' Tajweed course focuses on teaching students the basics of tajweed, so that they are able to properly apply the ruling of reciting the Quran. Students will learn the rulings for articulation of sukoon (silent letters), madd (elongating letters), and qalqala (bouncing letters).
Advanced
(Coming Soon)
$25 per session
Prerequesites: Completion of the Beginners' course
Students will learn advanced concepts in Tajweed, helping them to master the science of Tajweed. They will study a classical book in Tajweed, review a classical poem which describes all the rulings in detail, and recieve approval that they are able to fully articulate all the rules of the science.

Islamic Studies

Beginners
$15 per session
Prerequesites: None. This course is open to anyone!
The Beginners' Islamic Studies course focuses on teaching students the basics of Islam. Students will learn the 5 pillars of Islam, the 6 pillars of Eman, and the stories of the Prophets. This will give students a great foundation in understanding and practicing the religion, inshaAllah.
Advanced
(Coming Soon)
$15 per session
Prerequesites: Completion of the Beginners' course
Students will cover advanced topics in Islamic sciences, including: Aqeedah, Fiqh, and the science of Hadith. Students will learn the rulings regarding purification, salah, zakat, fasting, marriage, divorce, and buying and selling.
